Instructions

  • To move the player

  • Arrow up or "W": up
  • Arrow down or "S": down
  • Arrow right or "D": right
  • Arrow left or "A": left

  • To win stars

  • Collect gems to win stars
  • More than one gem can be on the same spot
  • The player needs 3 stars to win

  • To lose hearts

  • The player loses hearts when she/he gets hit by a bug
  • The player loses the game when she/he loses all the hearts
  • The player needs 3 stars to win

  • To win the game

  • The player needs 3 stars and at least 1 heart
  • The player wins when she/he gets into the water
  • The player needs 3 stars to win
